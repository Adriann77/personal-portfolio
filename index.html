Developed as part of the Nerdbord contract led by Jakub WÄ…sowski and Karolina Parysz.

This fullstack application empowers users to manage their expenses by scanning and analyzing receipts.

    The application offers several key features:

Tamagotchi

A modern recreation of the classic Tamagotchi game, bringing back the nostalgic virtual pet experience.

This project allows users to take care of their own virtual pet, Tamago, who can eat, play, and sleep, offering a delightful trip down memory lane.

  • Eight Different States: Tamago can enter eight unique states, making the gameplay dynamic and engaging.
  • Responsive Design: Enjoy the game on various devices with a fully responsive design.
  • Nostalgic Experience: Relive the classic Tamagotchi days with modern twists and enhancements.

Built with a focus on recreating the joy of the original Tamagotchi, this project brings the iconic virtual pet into the digital age.

Fischkapp

Fischkapp, an application developed to replicate the concept of fiches for educational purposes.

The design focuses on providing a user-friendly interface for creating, editing, and deleting fiches, making it easy for users to manage their educational resources.

    Key features of Fischkapp include:
  • User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive design that allows users to easily create, edit, and delete fiches.
  • Smooth Animations: The app includes smooth card-flipping animations to enhance the user experience.
  • Back-End Integration: Linked to a back-end system for storing existing fiches and adding new ones, ensuring your data is always accessible.

Fischkapp is designed to streamline the management of educational resources, providing a seamless experience for users looking to organize their learning materials.
